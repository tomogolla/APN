Pharmaceutical Workforce Development

Guiding Principle:

Growth of Africa’s pharmaceutical industry will be driven by qualified, competent and meaningfully engaged professionals in the pharmaceutical industry. It’s therefore imperative to invest in training for professionals to drive growth into this envisioned future.

Training

We offer technical, business & management courses to industry professionals and companies to address skills gaps.

Our Courses include:

  • Regulatory Affairs Training
  • Pharmacovigilance & Patient Safety
  • Pharmaceutical Quality Assurance
  • Pharmaceutical Supply Chain Management
  • Global Health Pharmacy
  • Digital Health & Data Science in Pharmacy
  • Pharmaceutical Management & Industry Leadership

Workshops & Industry Forums

We coordinate and facilitate industry workshops including policy caucuses geared towards:

  • Skills development for industry professionals & stakeholders.
  • Supporting product launches
  • Formulation of policy positions
  • Gathering market intelligence & insights on key topics.

We also offer customized trainings and workshops to organizations depending on their needs.
