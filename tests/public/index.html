About Us

The African Pharmaceutical Network (APN) is a continental network of pharmaceutical professionals, academics, researchers, and industry experts dedicated to strengthening pharmaceutical systems across Africa. Established in 2018, APN aims to enhance the capacity of African pharmaceutical professionals to contribute to improved health outcomes and sustainable development on the continent.

What We Do

Knowledge Sharing

Facilitate knowledge exchange among pharmaceutical professionals, stakeholders, and organizations across Africa.

Policy Shaping

Advocate for responsive and market-driven policies to strengthen the pharmaceutical sector in Africa.

Innovation Partnerships

Nurture partnerships aimed at fostering innovative approaches in pharmaceutical services and manufacturing.

Workforce Development

Promote workforce development initiatives to enhance skills and capabilities within the pharmaceutical industry.
